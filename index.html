Trajectory Generation BERT

This scalable trajectory imputation system was developed to address the challenge of sparse and incomplete trajectory datasets. Numerous applications, like pathfinding, traffic monitoring, location-based services, map inference, and urban planning, rely heavily on accurate trajectory data generated from GPS devices. However, due to limitations like bandwidth, storage constraints, etc, trajectory coordinates are sparse, leading to reduced accuracy.

This framework inserts realistic coordinates in a trajectory to enhance accuracy by mapping the trajectory imputation problem to the natural language processing(NLP) technique of finding the missing word by leveraging the widely used BERT LLM. It adapts to the unique characteristics of trajectories and doesn't require knowledge of the underlying road network or dense trajectory data.

Tech Stack: Python, NumPy, Pandas, Jupiter Notebook, Spark, BERT
